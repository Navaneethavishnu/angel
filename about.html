My Story

She proved her talent in academic which grabbed her the IET PATW South Asian Presenter Award which was published in Times of India on Aug 22,2015. The project then reached its next level through All India Radio Program. This exposure grabbed an opportunity to take part in Student Exchange Program, Italy. She was selected as the Indian Women for International Commission Later, She joined the IEEE special interested Group on Humanitarian Technology and initiated her role as the Project Coordinator for an award winning product funded by IEE ADSGAF BOBP-IGO, AVCE and successfully launched in Dec 2015.

She started her Professional work in Indian Institute of Technology Madras since February, 2016 under the mentorship of Dr. Ashok JhunJhunwala, IIT Madras. Angelin is the Member and Volunteer of WIIW, IEEE ADSF SIGHT & IEEE PW SIGHT, winner of IET SXCCE Best Extracurricular Award in 2014 , SXCCE Best Outgoing Student Award, in 2015, Young Woman Engineer Award from IET YP KKLN in 2016 and Best Team Leader Award from IEEE SIGHT in 2016.

When Angelin is passionate about something. She goes to extreme efforts to meet her passion. She has innovated a project for Kanyakumari Fishing Community, and has organized a five day summer Camp for students across India to bring awareness about problems in sea and fishing communities. She dedicates herself into the Maximus in order to attain a solid achievements
